Warning Signs to Watch For

Immediate Action Required

Burning Smell

Electrical burning smell from outlets, switches, or panel requires immediate professional attention.

Sparking or Arcing

Visible sparks from outlets or switches. Turn off circuit immediately and call electrician.

Electrical Shocks

Getting shocked when touching appliances or switches indicates dangerous grounding issues.

Warm/Hot Outlets

Outlets that feel warm or hot to touch are fire hazards. Stop using immediately.

Schedule Service Soon

Flickering Lights

Lights that flicker or dim, especially when appliances turn on, indicate circuit overload or loose connections.

Buzzing Sounds

Buzzing from outlets, switches, or breaker panel suggests loose connections or overloaded circuits.

Frequently Tripping Breakers

Breakers that trip regularly indicate overloaded circuits or electrical faults.

Discolored Outlets

Brown or black marks around outlets show overheating. Replace outlets and check wiring.
