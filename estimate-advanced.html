Our Process

We follow a simple, transparent process to ensure your electrical projects are completed efficiently and to your satisfaction.

1

Request an Estimate

Fill out our detailed estimate request form with information about your project. Add photos or videos to help us better understand your needs.

Tip: The more details and photos you provide, the more accurate your estimate will be!

2

Receive Your Detailed Estimate

Within 24 hours, you'll receive a comprehensive estimate that includes:

  • Detailed step-by-step work plan
  • Transparent materials list with pricing
  • Estimated completion time
  • Any potential variables that could affect the project

Our flat-rate pricing means no surprises – you know exactly what you'll pay before we begin.

3

Schedule Your Service

Once you approve the estimate, we'll schedule a convenient time for your electrical work. We offer flexible appointment times to minimize disruption to your day.

We confirm all service appointments 24 hours in advance and provide a 30-minute arrival window.

4

Professional Installation & Satisfaction

Our licensed electricians arrive on time, work efficiently, and clean up when finished. Before we leave, we'll walk you through the completed work and answer any questions.

All work is backed by our comprehensive 5-year warranty for your peace of mind.
