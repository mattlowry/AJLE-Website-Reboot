Our Story in Fairfax, Virginia

AJ Long Electric began in 1996 as a true family business in Northern Virginia, founded by AJ Long Sr. with a commitment to honest work and treating customers like neighbors. After years of seeing homeowners throughout Fairfax County frustrated with unreliable contractors and unclear pricing, AJ decided to build a company based on transparency, quality, and exceptional service.

Joined by his son, AJ Long Jr., the company has grown while maintaining those core family values. We are a father-and-son team at heart, bringing generations of experience and a personal touch to every job in Fairfax, Arlington, Alexandria and throughout the DMV area. We understand the importance of trust when inviting someone into your Northern Virginia home, which is why we focus on clear communication, meticulous workmanship, and leaving your space cleaner than we found it.

Today, AJ Long Electric is proud to be one of the most trusted and highly-rated residential electricians in Fairfax VA and the entire DMV area, with over 1,200 5-star reviews reflecting our dedication to customer satisfaction. We're your neighbors, and we treat every home we work in like our own.
