Specialized Electrical Solutions for Capitol Hill

AJ Long Electric brings over 15 years of specialized expertise to Capitol Hill's unique electrical landscape. As one of DC's oldest and most historic neighborhoods, Capitol Hill presents distinctive challenges with its blend of historic residential properties, government buildings, and vibrant commercial corridors.

From the iconic Victorian row houses along East Capitol Street to the government offices surrounding the Capitol complex, our licensed professionals understand the delicate balance between preserving architectural heritage and implementing modern electrical systems. We work closely with the Capitol Hill Historic District guidelines to ensure all electrical work maintains neighborhood character while providing modern functionality.

Our Capitol Hill electricians specialize in the specific challenges of this neighborhood, from knob-and-tube wiring replacement in historic homes to high-security electrical systems for government facilities and adaptive reuse solutions for commercial properties in historic buildings.
