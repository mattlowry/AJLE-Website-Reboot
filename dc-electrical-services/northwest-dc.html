Premium Electrical Solutions for Northwest DC

AJ Long Electric brings over 15 years of specialized expertise to Northwest DC's diverse electrical landscape. As the largest and most affluent quadrant of Washington DC, Northwest presents unique electrical challenges across its many distinguished neighborhoods and property types.

From the stately homes of Chevy Chase and Spring Valley to the historic residences of Cleveland Park and Woodley Park, our licensed professionals understand the specific requirements of these prestigious properties. We also provide specialized services for the area's luxury condominiums, educational institutions, and upscale commercial districts throughout Northwest DC.

Our Northwest DC electricians are experts in both high-end modern installations and sensitive historic renovations, delivering electrical solutions that combine luxury, reliability, and respect for architectural heritage throughout neighborhoods like Van Ness, Tenleytown, Friendship Heights, American University Park, and beyond.
