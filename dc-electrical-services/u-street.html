Powering U Street's Cultural Renaissance

AJ Long Electric understands the unique electrical demands of the U Street corridor. As the historic heart of DC's African American heritage and now a bustling center of culture, dining, and modern living, U Street presents diverse electrical challenges that our experienced team is equipped to handle.

From the historic Lincoln Theatre and Ben's Chili Bowl to modern luxury condominiums and trendy restaurants, we provide specialized electrical services that respect the area's cultural significance while meeting contemporary power demands. Our electricians have extensive experience with both historic preservation requirements and high-capacity commercial installations.

Whether you're renovating a historic rowhouse, updating a music venue's stage lighting, or installing EV charging in a modern condo building, AJ Long Electric delivers reliable, code-compliant electrical solutions tailored to U Street's unique character.
