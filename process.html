What Makes Our Process Different

We've refined our process over the years to provide an exceptional customer experience that sets us apart.

Photo & Video Estimates

Unlike many other electrical companies, we don't require an in-person visit for every estimate. You can upload photos and videos of your project area, allowing us to provide accurate estimates faster.

Transparent Flat-Rate Pricing

We provide detailed, itemized estimates with flat-rate pricing. You'll know exactly what you're paying for, with no hidden fees or surprise charges. Our pricing is competitive and transparent.

24-Hour Response Time

We respond to all estimate requests within 24 hours. We understand that electrical issues can be urgent, and we pride ourselves on our quick response times and efficient scheduling.

Single-Visit Completion

We plan our work meticulously to complete most projects in a single visit. Our trucks are well-stocked with quality materials, minimizing delays and return trips.

Clean Work Guarantee

We treat your home with respect. Our electricians wear boot covers, use drop cloths, and clean up thoroughly when the job is complete. We leave your home cleaner than we found it.

Thorough Final Inspection

Before we consider a job complete, we conduct a thorough inspection and walkthrough with you. We want to ensure you're 100% satisfied with our work and understand any new electrical features.

Common Questions About Our Process

Answers to frequently asked questions about working with AJ Long Electric.

How quickly can I get an estimate?

We provide estimates within 24 hours of receiving your request. For simple projects with clear photos or videos, we can often provide same-day estimates. More complex projects may require additional questions or a brief phone consultation.

Do I need to be home for the estimate?

No, one of the advantages of our process is that you don't need to be home for the initial estimate. Simply fill out our online form and upload photos/videos of the project area. If an in-person assessment is necessary, we'll let you know.

How long does the scheduling process take?

Once you approve an estimate, we can typically schedule your project within 1-2 weeks, depending on our current workload and the project scope. For urgent issues, we offer priority scheduling options. We also provide specific appointment times with a 30-minute arrival window.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and coordinate inspections with local authorities. Our estimates include permit fees, and we manage the entire permitting process to ensure your electrical work meets all code requirements and passes inspection.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the work?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely satisfied with any aspect of our work, we'll return to address your concerns at no additional cost. All our work is backed by a comprehensive 5-year warranty, giving you peace of mind that we stand behind our craftsmanship.
