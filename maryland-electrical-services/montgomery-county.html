Electrical Solutions for Montgomery County

AJ Long Electric has been serving Montgomery County for over 20 years, delivering tailored electrical services to meet the diverse needs of this dynamic region. From rewiring historic homes in Chevy Chase to installing smart home systems in Rockville’s modern developments, our licensed electricians are equipped to handle any residential project.

We specialize in preserving the architectural charm of Montgomery County’s older homes while ensuring their electrical systems meet modern safety and efficiency standards. For newer residences, we offer advanced solutions like EV charging stations and energy-efficient lighting to support the county’s eco-conscious communities.

Our service area covers all of Montgomery County, including Rockville, Bethesda, Silver Spring, Kensington, Chevy Chase, and surrounding neighborhoods. We are well-versed in local codes, HOA regulations, and the unique electrical requirements of the county’s varied housing stock.
