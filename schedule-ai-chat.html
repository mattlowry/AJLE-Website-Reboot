Frequently Asked Questions

How does scheduling with the AI assistant work?

Simply chat with our AI assistant about the electrical service you need. The assistant will ask for details like service type, location, and your contact information. Then it will show you available time slots to book your appointment. Once you select a time, the appointment is confirmed in our system.

Do I need to provide payment information?

No, payment is not required to schedule an appointment. Our technician will provide an estimate during the service visit, and payment is collected after the service is completed to your satisfaction.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment by calling our office at (703) 997-0026 or by using the links in your confirmation email. We ask that you provide at least 24 hours notice for any changes to your appointment.
