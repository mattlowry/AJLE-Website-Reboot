EV Charging Solutions We Recommend

Level 1 Charging

3-5 miles /hour
  • • Uses standard 120V outlet
  • • No special installation required
  • • Best for plug-in hybrids
  • • Overnight charging for short commutes
Best for: Minimal daily driving, backup charging
MOST POPULAR

Level 2 Charging

10-60 miles /hour
  • • Requires 240V dedicated circuit
  • • Professional installation needed
  • • Fully charges most EVs overnight
  • • Compatible with all EVs
Best for: Daily drivers, most homeowners

DC Fast Charging

60-200 miles /hour
  • • Commercial/public installations
  • • Rapid charging capability
  • • Requires special infrastructure
  • • Not typically residential
Best for: Commercial properties, fleet charging
