Professional Placement Guidelines

Key factors our experts consider for optimal ceiling fan performance.

Room Size & Fan Size

  • Rooms up to 75 sq ft: 29-36" fan
  • 76-144 sq ft: 36-42" fan
  • 144-225 sq ft: 44-50" fan
  • 225+ sq ft: 52" or larger fan

Height & Clearances

  • 7 feet minimum from floor to blades
  • 18 inches minimum from walls
  • 8-10 feet from ceiling for optimal airflow
  • Downrod length based on ceiling height
