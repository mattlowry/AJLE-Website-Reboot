Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about recessed lighting installation in Arlington homes.

How long does recessed lighting installation take in Arlington homes?

For a typical Arlington home, installing 6-10 recessed lights usually takes 1-2 days. The timeline depends on factors such as the complexity of your ceiling structure, whether you're replacing existing fixtures or adding new lights, and if we need to run new wiring. We'll provide a specific timeline estimate during your consultation.

Do I need a permit for recessed lighting installation in Arlington?

Yes, Arlington County typically requires electrical permits for recessed lighting installation. As licensed electricians, we handle all permitting requirements on your behalf as part of our service, ensuring your installation is code-compliant and inspected properly.

How many recessed lights do I need in my Arlington home?

The number of recessed lights depends on the size and function of your space. For Arlington kitchens, we typically recommend one light per 25 square feet for ambient lighting, with additional task lighting over work areas. For living rooms, one light per 50-100 square feet is common. During our in-home consultation, we'll determine the ideal number and placement for your specific space.

Can you install recessed lights in my Arlington condo with concrete ceilings?

Yes, we specialize in recessed lighting solutions for Arlington's many high-rise condos with concrete ceilings. We can utilize a variety of approaches, including shallow-depth LED fixtures, surface-mounted lights that mimic recessed fixtures, or creating a drop ceiling in strategic areas. Our experience with Arlington's urban housing stock ensures we can find the right solution for your specific building.

Do you offer smart lighting options for Arlington homes?

Absolutely! We specialize in smart lighting solutions that are popular in Arlington's tech-savvy community. We can install compatible dimmer switches and integrate your recessed lighting with systems like Lutron Caseta, Philips Hue, Samsung SmartThings, and Google Nest for voice control and smartphone app functionality. We'll discuss your smart home preferences during your consultation.
