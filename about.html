About AJ Long Electric

Get to know our family-owned company, our mission, and the dedicated team that makes AJ Long Electric one of the highest-rated electrical service providers in Northern Virginia, DC, and Maryland.

Our Story

AJ Long Electric began in 1996 as a true family business, founded by John Long with a commitment to honest work and treating customers like neighbors. After years of seeing homeowners frustrated with unreliable contractors and unclear pricing, John decided to build a company based on transparency, quality, and exceptional service.

Joined by his son, Matt Long, the company has grown while maintaining those core family values. We are a father-and-son team at heart, bringing generations of experience and a personal touch to every job. We understand the importance of trust when inviting someone into your home, which is why we focus on clear communication, meticulous workmanship, and leaving your space cleaner than we found it.

Today, AJ Long Electric is proud to be one of the most trusted and highly-rated residential electricians in the DMV area, with over 1,200 5-star reviews reflecting our dedication to customer satisfaction.
