Frequently Asked Questions

Do USB outlets use power when nothing is plugged in?

Modern USB outlets use minimal standby power (less than 0.1 watts) when not charging devices, making them very energy efficient.

Can USB outlets charge laptops?

USB-C outlets with Power Delivery can charge many laptops, tablets, and other devices that support USB-C charging up to 60 watts.

Are USB outlets safe for all devices?

Yes, quality USB outlets include smart charging technology that automatically detects device requirements and provides appropriate power levels safely.
