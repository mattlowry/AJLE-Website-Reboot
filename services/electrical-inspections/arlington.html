Our Inspection Process

Thorough, systematic electrical inspections for Arlington properties

1

Visual Inspection

We begin with a comprehensive visual examination of your electrical system, including panels, wiring, outlets, fixtures, and visible components throughout your Arlington property.

2

Testing & Measurements

Using specialized equipment, we test circuits, measure loads, verify grounding, check GFCI/AFCI functionality, and ensure all components operate safely.

3

Code Compliance Review

We evaluate your system against current Arlington County electrical codes and national standards, identifying any violations or safety concerns.

4

Detailed Report

You receive a comprehensive inspection report with findings, photographs, code references, and prioritized recommendations for any necessary repairs or upgrades.
