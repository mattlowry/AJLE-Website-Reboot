Frequently Asked Questions

Can automation be added to an existing home?

Yes, most homes can be retrofitted with automation systems. We assess your current electrical system and recommend the best approach for integration.

Do I need special wiring for home automation?

Many smart devices work with existing wiring, but some systems may require neutral wires or dedicated circuits for optimal performance.

How reliable are home automation systems?

Modern automation systems are highly reliable with backup capabilities and multiple control methods to ensure consistent operation.
