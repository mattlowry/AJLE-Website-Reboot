Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use an extension cord for my dryer?

No, dryers and other 240V appliances must be hardwired or connected directly to a dedicated outlet. Extension cords pose serious fire and safety risks.

Do I need a permit for appliance wiring?

Most appliance wiring projects require electrical permits, especially for new circuits or 240V installations. We handle permit applications and inspections.

How do I know if my panel can handle a new appliance?

We perform electrical load calculations to ensure your panel has adequate capacity. Panel upgrades may be needed for high-amperage appliances.
