Pool Electrical Safety Requirements

GFCI Protection

All pool electrical circuits must be protected with Ground Fault Circuit Interrupters to prevent electrical shock.

Equipment Bonding

Metal components must be properly bonded to eliminate potential electrical differences and shock hazards.

Clearance Requirements

Electrical equipment must maintain proper distances from pool edges and water features per NEC guidelines.

Code Compliance

All installations meet National Electrical Code Article 680 and local jurisdiction requirements.
