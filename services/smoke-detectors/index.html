Types of Detectors We Install

Ionization Smoke Detectors

Best for detecting fast-flaming fires. Quick response to fires with flames and minimal smoke.

Photoelectric Smoke Detectors

Ideal for smoldering fires. Better at detecting slow-burning fires with heavy smoke.

Dual-Sensor Detectors

Combines both ionization and photoelectric technologies for comprehensive fire protection.

Carbon Monoxide Detectors

Detects deadly carbon monoxide gas from furnaces, water heaters, and appliances.

Proper Detector Placement

Smoke Detector Locations

  • • Inside every bedroom
  • • Outside each sleeping area
  • • On every level of the home
  • • At the top of stairways
  • • In hallways longer than 40 feet
  • • Away from air vents and windows

Carbon Monoxide Detector Locations

  • • Near sleeping areas
  • • On every level of the home
  • • Near attached garages
  • • Near fuel-burning appliances
  • • At least 5 feet from fuel-burning sources
  • • Away from high humidity areas

Detector Maintenance

Essential Maintenance Schedule

Monthly:

  • • Test all detectors using test button
  • • Check for chirping or low battery warnings
  • • Verify LED lights are functioning

Annually:

  • • Replace backup batteries
