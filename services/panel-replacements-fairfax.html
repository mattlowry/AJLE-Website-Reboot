Panel Replacement Services

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home's electrical system. When it's outdated or malfunctioning, it can pose serious safety risks and cause operational issues.

Why Consider a Panel Upgrade?

In Fairfax's older homes, many electrical panels are outdated and weren't designed to handle modern electrical demands. Today's homes use 3-5 times more electricity than homes built just a few decades ago, making panel upgrades a necessity for many homeowners.

At AJ Long Electric, we specialize in replacing outdated panels with modern, high-capacity alternatives that deliver improved safety, reliability, and capacity for your growing electrical needs.

Increased Capacity

Support more appliances and devices with improved amperage

Enhanced Safety

Modern circuit breakers provide better protection against electrical hazards

Increased Home Value

Updated electrical systems are an attractive selling point for potential buyers

Signs You Need a Panel Replacement:

  • Your panel is 25+ years old or is a Federal Pacific, Zinsco, or fuse box
  • Circuit breakers frequently trip when using multiple appliances
  • Lights dim or flicker when major appliances turn on
  • Your panel feels warm or you notice buzzing sounds
  • You're planning to add major appliances or an EV charger
  • You're planning a home renovation or addition
  • You smell burning or see scorching around the panel

Benefits of a Panel Upgrade

A modern electrical panel provides numerous advantages for your Fairfax home

Improved Safety

Modern panels significantly reduce fire risks and electrical hazards with advanced circuit protection. They comply with current electrical codes and include AFCI/GFCI protection.

Increased Capacity

Support more appliances and devices with 200-amp service instead of outdated 60 or 100-amp panels. Add new circuits for home offices, kitchen upgrades, or EV chargers without worry.

Reliable Power

Eliminate annoying circuit trips, flickering lights, and power inconsistencies. Modern panels deliver stable, consistent power to all your home's systems and appliances.

Increased Home Value

Updated electrical systems are attractive to potential buyers and can increase property value. Modern panels can also reduce insurance premiums in many cases.

Future Expansion

Prepare your home for future electrical needs and technologies. Modern panels provide room for expansion as your electrical needs grow over time.

Better Organization

Modern panels feature clear labeling and better organization of circuits. Makes maintenance, troubleshooting, and future electrical work much easier.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about panel replacements in Fairfax, VA

How long does a panel replacement take?

A typical panel replacement in Fairfax takes 6-8 hours to complete. However, this can vary depending on the complexity of your electrical system and any additional work required. The power to your home will be off during this time, so we recommend planning accordingly. We'll give you a specific timeframe when we provide your estimate.

Do I need a permit to replace my electrical panel in Fairfax?

Yes, electrical panel replacements in Fairfax County require a permit and inspection. As a licensed electrical contractor in Virginia, AJ Long Electric handles all permitting paperwork and schedules the required inspections on your behalf. This is included in our service price, so you don't need to worry about navigating the permitting process.

How do I know if I need 100 amps or 200 amps?

For most modern homes in Fairfax, we recommend a 200-amp panel. This provides ample capacity for typical household electrical needs plus room for future additions. Smaller homes with minimal electrical demands might be fine with 100 amps. During our assessment, we'll evaluate your current electrical usage, any planned additions (like EV chargers or new appliances), and recommend the appropriate size for your specific needs.

Will my homeowners insurance be affected by a panel upgrade?

In many cases, replacing an outdated electrical panel can potentially lower your homeowners insurance premiums. Some insurance companies may even require panel upgrades if you have certain outdated panel types, like Federal Pacific or Zinsco. After your panel replacement, we provide documentation that you can submit to your insurance company, which may help reduce your rates due to the decreased fire risk.

What brands of electrical panels do you install?

We primarily install Square D, Eaton, and Siemens electrical panels, which are among the most reliable and respected brands in the industry. We select panels based on quality, safety features, and long-term reliability. Our electricians will discuss the specific options available and recommend the best solution for your home during the assessment.

Is financing available for panel replacements?

Yes, we offer financing options to help make your panel replacement more affordable. We partner with reputable financing companies to offer flexible payment plans with competitive rates. During your estimate, we can discuss the available financing options and help you apply if you're interested. Many of our Fairfax customers appreciate the ability to spread the cost of this important safety upgrade over time.
