Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use any dimmer with LED lights?

No, LEDs require specific dimmer switches designed for electronic low voltage (ELV) or LED compatibility to prevent flickering and ensure proper function.

Do I need neutral wires for smart dimmers?

Most smart dimmers require a neutral wire for power. We can assess your existing wiring and install neutral wires if needed for smart dimmer installation.

Can dimmers work with ceiling fans?

Standard dimmers should never be used with ceiling fans. We install special fan speed controls designed specifically for fan motor control.
