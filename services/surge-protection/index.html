What is Whole-House Surge Protection?

Whole-house surge protection is a comprehensive electrical safety system installed at your main electrical panel that protects all circuits in your home from voltage spikes. Unlike plug-in surge protectors that only protect individual outlets, whole-house systems provide complete protection for:

  • • Major appliances (refrigerators, washers, dryers)
  • • HVAC systems and smart thermostats
  • • Home entertainment systems
  • • Computers and home office equipment
  • • Smart home devices and security systems
  • • LED lighting and dimmer switches
  • • Electric vehicle charging stations

Types of Surge Protection We Install

Type 1 Protection

Installed at the service entrance, protecting against external surges from lightning and utility issues.

Type 2 Protection

Installed at the main panel, providing whole-house protection from both external and internal surges.

Type 3 Protection

Point-of-use protection for sensitive electronics, working in conjunction with whole-house systems.
