Step 3: Cable Management

Plan for clean, organized cable routing and future expansion

Power Cables

  • Dedicated 20A circuits for high-power equipment
  • Isolated ground circuits for sensitive AV gear
  • Surge protection at panel and equipment
  • Separate analog and digital power feeds

Signal Cables

  • HDMI 2.1 cables for 4K/8K content
  • Speaker wire routing (14-12 AWG)
  • Ethernet for streaming and smart devices
  • Coaxial for cable/satellite signals

Conduit Strategy

  • 2" conduit for equipment rack connections
  • 1" conduit for individual device runs
  • Pull strings for future cable additions
  • Access panels for maintenance
