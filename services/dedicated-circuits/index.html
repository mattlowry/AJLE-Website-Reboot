What Are Dedicated Circuits?

Dedicated circuits are electrical circuits that serve only one appliance or outlet. They provide the full power capacity needed for high-demand equipment without sharing with other devices.

  • Prevents circuit overloading and tripping
  • Ensures optimal appliance performance
  • Required by electrical code for many appliances
  • Improves electrical safety and reliability
