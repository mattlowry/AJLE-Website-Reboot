Why Install a Backup Generator?

Power outages in Northern Virginia can last hours or even days, especially during storms. A backup generator ensures your family's comfort, safety, and security when the grid goes down.

  • Automatic power restoration within seconds
  • Keep refrigerators, medical equipment, and security systems running
  • Maintain heating and cooling during extended outages
  • Increase home value and insurance benefits
