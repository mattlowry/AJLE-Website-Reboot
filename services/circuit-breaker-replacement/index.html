Signs You Need Circuit Breaker Replacement

Frequent Tripping

Breakers that trip repeatedly indicate overloading, short circuits, or worn-out breakers that need replacement.

Burning Smell

Electrical burning odors from the panel indicate dangerous arcing or overheating that requires immediate attention.

Hot Panel or Breakers

Warm or hot breakers and panel surfaces indicate dangerous electrical problems requiring professional repair.

Won't Reset

Breakers that won't stay reset or feel loose when switched indicate internal damage requiring replacement.

Old Age

Breakers over 20-30 years old should be replaced as part of panel upgrades and safety improvements.

Visible Damage

Scorching, discoloration, or physical damage to breakers requires immediate replacement for safety.
