Kitchen Electrical Code Requirements

We ensure all kitchen electrical work meets current electrical codes for safety and compliance.

Required Circuits

  • Two 20-amp circuits: For countertop outlets
  • Dedicated circuits: For dishwasher, garbage disposal
  • 240V circuit: For electric range/cooktop
  • Lighting circuit: Separate from outlet circuits

Safety Requirements

  • GFCI protection: All countertop and wet area outlets
  • Outlet spacing: Max 4 feet between outlets
  • Height requirements: Proper placement above counters
  • Proper grounding: All circuits and equipment
