Why Replace Aluminum Wiring?

Many homes built between 1965-1973 contain aluminum branch circuit wiring, which poses significant fire and safety risks. Our licensed electricians specialize in safely replacing aluminum wiring with modern copper throughout Northern Virginia.

Aluminum wiring expands and contracts more than copper, leading to loose connections, overheating, and potential fires. Insurance companies and safety experts recommend complete replacement for maximum protection.

Safety Alert: Homes with aluminum wiring are 55 times more likely to have fire conditions at outlets and switches.

Warning Signs of Aluminum Wiring Problems

Warm Outlets

Wall plates or switches that feel warm to the touch

Discoloration

Brown marks around outlets, switches, or panels

Flickering Lights

Lights dimming when appliances turn on
